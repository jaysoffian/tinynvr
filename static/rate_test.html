playbackRate drift test (Safari / WebKit)

Measures whether video.playbackRate adjustments close to 1.0 are honored by WebKit on MSE-less <video> playback of a real NVR segment. For each target rate, lets playback run ~8 seconds of wall time and computes the effective rate as Δvideo.currentTime / Δwall. Results are POSTed to /api/debug/log (pull with curl .../api/debug/log).
